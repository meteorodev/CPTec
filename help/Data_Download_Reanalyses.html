Download: Reanalyses

All the reanalysis datasets are from the NCEP-NCAR CDAS-1 Reanalysis. CPT provides access to monthly data for 1949 to date. The data are typically updated towards the end of the first week of each month. The following variables are provided:
  • mean sea-level pressure (MSLP)
  • geopotential heights at various pressure levels
  • horizontal wind components at various pressure levels
If multiple pressure levels, and / or U and V wind components together, are selected, CPT will read these as multiple fields. The reanalysis data are likely to be most useful in diagnostic (i.e., with Options ~ Data Synchronous Predictors switched on) rather than predictive analyses.
